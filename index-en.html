About Us

A Modern Language School with a Personal Approach

Ally Lingua is an English language school that helps you unlock your potential and reach your goals — whether that's career progress, an exam, a move abroad, or simply the confidence to hold a conversation.

Lessons take place both at our learning centre and online via Zoom. Every lesson covers all four language skills — speaking, reading, listening and writing — with a strong focus on real-life conversation practice.

We focus on:

  • Speaking practice from lesson one — not 'somewhere down the line'
  • Programmes built around a specific goal — career, exams, relocation or travel
  • Flexible scheduling — early morning or late evening lessons
  • Teachers holding international TEFL, CELTA and DELTA certificates
  • Regular progress reviews and honest feedback

Our goal is simple: confidence and clarity in communication, in every single lesson.
