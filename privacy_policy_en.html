Legal Information

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Ally Lingua is the teaching practice of a private English tutor. I am not a registered legal entity or company — I personally manage the website, bookings and correspondence with students. Some lessons (for example, certain group classes) may be taught by another teacher engaged by me as a freelancer under contract. This Policy explains what data I collect through the website, why, and how I use it, including cases where another teacher delivers the lesson.

2. Who Processes the Data

Tutor / Data Controller: Ally Lingua (private tutor)

Place of Business: Ireland, EU

Email: ally.lingua@gmail.com

I am personally the data controller. For some lessons, I engage other freelance teachers working under the Ally Lingua brand. They only receive access to the minimum data necessary (see Section 4) and are bound by a written confidentiality agreement covering student data.

As I am based in the EU, data processing is subject to the requirements of the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

3. What Data I Collect

When you fill in the booking form on the website, I collect:

  • Full name — to address you personally
  • Phone number — to contact you and arrange lessons
  • Email — for correspondence, confirmations and lesson offers
  • English level — to recommend a suitable programme
  • Learning goal — to understand how I can help you

4. How I Use This Data

Only I personally use the data from the form, for the following purposes:

  • To contact you about your enquiry
  • To organise a trial or regular lesson
  • To send you relevant offers about lessons and programmes

I Do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use it for purposes unrelated to English teaching
  • Share it with any services or people other than those needed to deliver your lessons (for example, email, or the teacher of your group)

Who Receives the Minimum Necessary Data

If your lesson is taught by another teacher engaged by me as a freelancer, I only pass on what is needed to run the lesson: the student's name, English level, and the topic or programme of the lesson. Your contact details (phone, email) and correspondence with you stay with me and are not shared. All teachers I work with are bound by a written confidentiality agreement covering student data and are not permitted to use it beyond the scope of delivering lessons.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent — you voluntarily provide your data through the website form
  • Legitimate interest — to respond to your enquiry and organise a lesson
  • Consent to receive offers — you may unsubscribe from offers at any time by emailing me

6. Where and How Data Is Stored

Only I personally have full access to your data. I store it in my personal email and notes, without passing it on to any agencies, analytics platforms or advertising services. The website does not use third-party trackers or advertising pixels.

Teachers engaged to deliver specific lessons only see the minimal set of data described in Section 4, and only for the duration of their collaboration on your group or course.

7. Retention Period

  • Active enquiries: for as long as we are in contact about lessons
  • Current students: throughout the course of study, plus up to one year after it ends
  • Enquiries with no follow-up: deleted within 12 months

You can ask me to delete your data earlier at any time — simply email me.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data I hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict the processing of your data
  • Receive your data in a structured format
  • Object to processing, including receiving offers
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please email me at ally.lingua@gmail.com. If you believe your rights have been infringed, you may also contact the Irish Data Protection Commission (Data Protection Commission, dataprotection.ie).

9. Cookies

The website may use basic technical cookies necessary for the pages to function correctly. The website does not use advertising cookies or third-party analytics or tracking systems. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is not transferred outside the EU/EEA and is stored only by me personally.

11. Children's Privacy

Some of my programmes are designed for children. If a child is under 16, booking lessons requires the consent and contact details of a parent or legal guardian — the form must be completed by an adult.

12. Changes to This Policy

I may update this page from time to time. The date at the top of the page always reflects the latest version.

13. Contact

Email: ally.lingua@gmail.com

Place of Business: Ireland, EU
